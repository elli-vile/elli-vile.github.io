Privacy Policy – Dataview

Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Dataview (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects information during your use of the application. Dataview is currently under development.

1. Information We Collect

  • OAuth tokens provided by TikTok for authentication.
  • TikTok profile data allowed by the TikTok Display API (e.g., username, avatar, public stats).
  • User-created content such as notes, campaigns, tags, and ideas stored locally in the app using Drift local database.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No sensitive personal information.
  • No location tracking.
  • No behavior tracking across apps or services.

3. Local Storage

All user-created campaign data is stored locally on your device. This local data belongs to you and remains private unless you choose to sync with an external server.

4. Optional Cloud Sync

If enabled, Dataview syncs selected data with our server. This data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). We do not access or analyze individual user content.

5. TikTok Data

TikTok data accessed through the API is used only to display analytics and insights. It is not shared or sold.

6. Third-Party Access

We do not share your data with any third parties unless required by law.

7. Data Removal

You may delete your data by uninstalling the app or contacting us for assistance.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as Dataview evolves. Updates will be posted on this page.
